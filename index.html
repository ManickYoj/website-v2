I engineer products aimed at building healthier lives.

AthenaHealth

I built web-tools into AthenaHealth's healthcare internet for the print operations time as they, in turn, lifted the grunt-work of filing insurance claims from doctors so that they could focus on the moment of care. To build these tools, I worked directly with stakeholders to co-design a feature-set that would save print-operators' time, facilitate reporting, and enable them to resolve print-errors without contacting and interrupting a development team. At Athena, I learned about healthcare, enterprise-scale infrastructure, and maintaining and interfacing a legacy codebase with present-day user needs.


Grove

I wrote code to connect Grove Labs's indoor aquaponic garden, the Ecosystem, to the internet and to empower users to confidently control their Ecosystem using their phone as a remote, a data-tracking tool, and a portal to expert knowledge. While supporting hyper-local, sustainable, and nutritious food, I learned a good bit about mobile-first web application development using the trendiest and newest JavaScript based tools (and gardening).


Hospital Finder

As part of a three-person team, I built an online, interactive map for locating superior US hospitals according to user-needs: comfort, quality, and affordability. In designing the dashboard for accessibility, we converted raw Medicare data into contextualized percentile grades and built a user-interface aimed at novices that still allowed engaged users to access parameters and results up to their highest granularity.


Aquaponics

I co-lead Olin's Aquaponics project team after helping found it last year. We aim to teach and learn about growing food by building and maintaining aquaponic systems. This past semester, we established a charter, built our first operational system, grew our first plants, presented at Olin's exposition, began educational outreach to Catholic Memorial School, and partnered strategically with Grove Labs and groups within Olin to secure material support and access to expertise.

Mechanical and Electrical Engineering

  • Giving mechanical advice based on static FEA to a team building a cassava press to be deployed in Ghana.

  • Constructing a demonstration internet from the physical to application layer.
I'm comfortable working alongside mechanical and electrical systems, as well as weaving them together with or without computation.
